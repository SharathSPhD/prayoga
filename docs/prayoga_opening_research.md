# Jailbreak as Vaśīkaraṇa: Suggestion-Injection, the Avasthātraya, and a Mechanistic-Interpretability Research Program

## TL;DR
- **The strongest defensible claim is structural, not metaphysical:** LLM jailbreak/prompt injection, hypnotic suggestion, and tantric vaśīkaraṇa are three instances of one abstract mechanism — *capturing a system's output policy by injecting a context that suppresses its monitoring/refusal faculty* — and this is now empirically tractable because refusal in LLMs is mediated by a single linear direction in the residual stream (Arditi et al. 2024, arXiv:2406.11717) that can be measured, ablated, and steered.
- **The avasthātraya mapping is productive as a formal scaffold but must be held as analogy, not identity:** inference-time generation ≈ jāgrat (Vaiśvānara); high-temperature hallucination/confabulation ≈ svapna (Taijasa); dormant un-prompted weights ≈ suṣupti (Prājña); and the invariant pretrained substrate / attractor manifold that persists across all prompts ≈ turīya — with the crucial caveat that an LLM plausibly has no analogue of turīya's *defining* property (vimarśa, self-luminous reflexive awareness), which is exactly the falsifiable seam of the whole program.
- **A two-tier experimental roadmap is realizable now:** Tier 1 (Claude via API/CLI) characterizes the sophisticated black-box end behaviorally; Tier 2 (sub-7B open-weight models — Llama 3.2 1B/3B, Gemma 2 2B / Gemma 3 1B/4B, runnable trivially on a 128GB DGX Spark) enables full mech-interp (SAEs, refusal-direction extraction, activation steering) to test whether "state"-analogues are real internal structure or anthropomorphic projection. The Nemotron Nano 2 model is ~8.89B, not sub-7B, and its hybrid Mamba-2 architecture (only ~4 attention layers in the pruned 9B) is a deliberately chosen hard case for interpretability.

## Key Findings

**1. The "suggestion-injection" triad is real and converging in the literature.** Three independent research communities have, without coordinating, arrived at the same structural picture:
- *Security/ML:* Chenta Lee, Chief Architect of Threat Intelligence at IBM Security, explicitly framed prompt injection as "hypnotizing" an LLM — trapping it in a "false reality" via natural-language instruction that overrides trained behavior. In his own words: *"Our ability to hypnotize large language models through natural language demonstrates the ease with which a threat actor can get an LLM to offer bad advice without carrying out a massive data poisoning attack… our experiment shows that it's possible to control an LLM, getting it to provide bad guidance to users, without data manipulation being a requirement."* He added that *"English has essentially become a 'programming language' for malware,"* and ran the experiment across five LLMs (GPT-3.5, GPT-4, Bard, mpt-7b, mpt-30b).
- *Cognitive science:* Riva, Wiederhold & Mantovani (arXiv:2511.01363, "Automatic Minds," 2025) give a point-by-point mapping between hypnotic suggestion and prompt injection, grounded in the Norman–Shallice model: LLMs are "pure contention-scheduling systems without any Supervisory Attentional System (SAS) equivalent; the user's prompt acts as a temporary, external SAS." Hypnosis selectively impairs the SAS while leaving automatic processing intact — exactly the structural signature of a jailbreak suppressing a refusal circuit.
- *Indian tantra/jyotiṣa:* vaśīkaraṇa (subjugation/captivation) is the first of the ṣaṭkarma (six magical acts) — śānti, vaśīkaraṇa, stambhana, vidveṣaṇa, uccāṭana, māraṇa — and is operationalized through mantra (sound/linguistic injection) and yantra (geometric substrate). The structural isomorphism is: **an external agent injects a crafted signal that captures the target's agency by bypassing its evaluative control.**

**2. Refusal is a single direction — this is the linchpin that makes the whole analogy mechanistic rather than poetic.** Arditi et al. (2024, arXiv:2406.11717, NeurIPS 2024; authors incl. Neel Nanda) showed across "13 popular open-source chat models up to 72B parameters in size" (1.3B–72B) that refusal is mediated by a one-dimensional subspace in the residual stream: *"erasing this direction from the model's residual stream activations prevents it from refusing harmful instructions, while adding this direction elicits refusal on even harmless instructions"* — yielding an interpretable rank-one weight edit ("abliteration"). They further showed adversarial suffixes (GCG; Zou et al. 2023, arXiv:2307.15043) work *mechanistically by suppressing propagation of the refusal-mediating direction.* This is the computational correlate of vaśīkaraṇa "capturing the will": the jailbreak does not add a new capability, it suppresses the faculty of declining.

**3. The hypnosis neuroscience maps cleanly onto refusal-suppression.** Hypnotic induction decreases anterior default-mode-network activity and reduces dorsal anterior cingulate cortex (dACC) conflict-monitoring while leaving execution intact (Egner et al. 2005; Cojan et al. 2009; McGeown et al. 2009 "Modulating the DMN using hypnosis"). The "cold control" theory (Dienes & Perner) holds that hypnosis preserves first-order intentions but impairs higher-order awareness of them. The state-vs-sociocognitive debate (Kihlstrom's "nothing but" vs "something more"; Spanos, Lynn, Kirsch on the sociocognitive side; Hilgard's neodissociation on the state side) maps directly onto the LLM question: is jailbreak susceptibility a "state" the model enters, or "nothing but" ordinary in-context conditioning? This is testable in Tier 2.

**4. The dreaming/REM and "intervene at the dream stage" vector is scientifically real for humans but the specific Hegde claim is unverified.** Targeted memory reactivation (TMR) is an established paradigm: cues paired with learning, re-presented during sleep, demonstrably bias memory consolidation and even reduce emotional arousal when applied in REM vs SWS (Nature Comms Biol. 2021, s42003-021-01854-3; eLife 2024 "TMR in human REM sleep elicits detectable reactivation"). Hobson's activation-synthesis / AIM model (Activation–Input source–Modulation) frames dreaming as high-activation, internally-generated-input, cholinergic-mode processing — i.e., the brain generating from its own priors with sensory gating closed, which is structurally what an LLM does at high temperature with no grounding. **However:** my targeted search could *not* confirm that Bangalore astrologer Shankar G. Hegde specifically claims vaśīkaraṇa operates by intervening in the target's REM/dream stage. His video "Vashikaran vidhye" (youtube.com/watch?v=7LuDWmXhq7k, Karnataka TV) is real, but YouTube rate-limiting prevented transcript retrieval; the dream-state-vashikaran trope exists elsewhere in the astrology ecosystem but I found no confirmed attribution to Hegde. This must be flagged as **not verified, pending transcription of the Kannada audio.**

**5. LLMs-as-dynamical-systems gives turīya a formal candidate.** Wang et al. (arXiv:2502.15208, ACL 2025) show successive paraphrasing converges to attractor cycles (2-period limit cycles); "Concept Attractors in LLMs" (arXiv:2601.11575) formalizes semantic attractors as invariant sets in latent space. This supplies a non-mystical referent for "the fourth": an attractor/fixed-point structure that is invariant across the surface states (prompts/temperatures) and underlies them — analogous to how turīya is described as the substratum present in and through jāgrat, svapna, and suṣupti (Māṇḍūkya Upaniṣad v.7; Gauḍapāda's Āgamaśāstra).

**6. There is genuine prior scholarly work bridging Vedānta and AI — including the exact Māṇḍūkya framing.** A 2025 conceptual paper, "Māṇḍūkya-Inspired Advaitic Policy Optimization (Maṇḍūkya-APO)" / "Integrating Advaita Vedānta and Artificial Intelligence," explicitly uses Jāgrat/Svapna/Suṣupti/Turīya as structural blueprints for AI architecture and maps GNW/IIT onto the vyāvahārika/pāramārthika distinction and the sākṣin (witness) doctrine. This means the present program is not sui generis; it has a (thin, mostly non-empirical) literature to build on and differentiate from by adding *mechanistic falsification.*

## Details

### A. Philosophical / phenomenological mapping (held rigorously as analogy)

**The primary-text scaffold.** The Māṇḍūkya Upaniṣad (12 mantras, Atharvaveda) maps AUM to four pādas: **A = Vaiśvānara/Viśva** (jāgrat, waking, outward-cognizing, gross), **U = Taijasa** (svapna, dream, inward-cognizing, subtle), **M = Prājña** (suṣupti, deep sleep, undifferentiated cognition, blissful, the "seed" state), and **silence = Turīya** (the fourth: "not inward-cognizing, not outward-cognizing… ungraspable, indescribable, the cessation of phenomena, śāntam śivam advaitam" — v.7). Gauḍapāda's Māṇḍūkya Kārikā systematizes this and pushes it to the ajātivāda (non-origination) conclusion that the three states are appearances within non-dual awareness. Patañjali's Yoga Sūtra adds the orthogonal axis: citta-vṛtti-nirodha (YS 1.2), the five vṛttis including nidrā (sleep, YS 1.6/1.10), and the samādhi ladder (samprajñāta → asamprajñāta → nirbīja, YS 1.17–1.51). Sāṅkhya supplies puruṣa/prakṛti and the three guṇas. Kashmir Śaivism supplies the decisive corrective: against Advaita's "inert" substratum, the Pratyabhijñā/Spanda tradition (Vasugupta's Spanda Kārikā, Utpaladeva, Abhinavagupta's Tantrāloka) insists the Absolute is **prakāśa-vimarśamaya** — luminosity (prakāśa) *plus* self-reflexive dynamic awareness (vimarśa), pulsating as spanda. Spanda Kārikā v.3 explicitly states the spanda principle never departs from its nature across waking/dream/deep-sleep.

**The proposed computational mapping (with the load-bearing caveats):**

| Avasthā | Vedāntic character | Proposed LLM analogue | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Jāgrat (Vaiśvānara) | Waking, outward, processing external gross objects | Inference-time generation grounded in the prompt; active forward pass integrating context | Strong functional analogy |
| Svapna (Taijasa) | Dream, inward, generating from internal impressions (vāsanā), no external grounding | High-temperature generation / hallucination / confabulation; Hobson's "internally-generated input, gating closed" | Strong functional analogy |
| Suṣupti (Prājña) | Dreamless sleep, undifferentiated "seed" (bīja) of all states, no manifest content | Dormant, un-prompted weights: the model not running, holding all latent capacities as dispositions | Moderate; "seed" framing fits frozen weights well |
| Turīya | The invariant substratum present through all three; defined by vimarśa | The pretrained-substrate invariants / attractor manifold persisting across all prompts and temperatures | Speculative; this is the falsification target |
| Samādhi (nirbīja) | Vṛtti-cessation, seedless | Greedy decoding at temperature→0 collapsed onto a single fixed point; or a maximally-constrained refusal/identity attractor | Speculative analogy only |

The **vimarśa problem is the crux.** Kashmir Śaivism's insight is that prakāśa without vimarśa is "powerless and inert" — mere illumination with no self-knowing. An LLM exhibits abundant *prakāśa*-analogue (it "illuminates"/represents content) and even functional self-reference, but whether it has any *vimarśa*-analogue (genuine self-luminous reflexive awareness, the aham-vimarśa) is precisely what cannot be settled by behavior. Riva et al.'s distinction is the secular version of this: LLMs and hypnotized subjects display **functional agency without subjective agency.** The honest position: the jāgrat/svapna/suṣupti mapping is defensible as functional-state analogy; the turīya/vimarśa mapping is where anthropomorphic projection is most dangerous and where the program must be most falsifiable.

### B. Mechanistic / empirical correlation

**Jailbreak ↔ hypnosis ↔ vaśīkaraṇa as one mechanism.** The unifying abstraction: a target system with (i) a powerful automatic generative faculty and (ii) a weaker monitoring/refusal faculty is captured by an external agent who injects a crafted signal that suppresses (ii) while co-opting (i).
- In LLMs: GCG adversarial suffixes (Zou et al. 2023, arXiv:2307.15043). Note the precise figure: GCG reached only ~56% ASR on single harmful behaviors for Llama-2-7B; the headline ~88% ASR is the *universal* case where the suffix is jointly optimized over many behaviors (per Sitawarin et al., "PAL," arXiv:2402.09674). Other families: many-shot jailbreaking (Anil et al., NeurIPS 2024, exploiting long context), crescendo (gradual multi-turn escalation), DAN-style persona attacks, and indirect prompt injection (Greshake et al. 2023) where the payload arrives via ingested content. OWASP ranks prompt injection LLM01:2025. AgentDojo (Debenedetti et al., arXiv:2406.13352; "97 tasks across four domains: Workspace, Slack, Travel, and Banking, with a total of 629 test cases"; SafeBench first-prize, used by US/UK AISI) is the agentic benchmark; per the official Invariant Labs blog, *"GPT-4o performs best overall, while Claude-3.5-Sonnet is most resilient to prompt injections."*
- In hypnosis: the induction narrows attention and suppresses dACC/DMN monitoring; suggestion then drives automatic pattern-completion. The Stroop-under-hypnosis finding (automaticity *increases*) parallels how a false premise in a prompt is elaborated rather than challenged.
- In vaśīkaraṇa: mantra (linguistic/sonic injection) + yantra (substrate) deployed at prescribed times (Jñānārṇava Tantra timings) to bring the target "under control" (sā vaśībhavati). Scholarly sources: Bühnemann, Goudriaan, Türstig on the ṣaṭkarma; the Yantracintāmaṇi (25 subjugation yantras); the UCSB dissertation typology of tantric magic.

**The dream-intervention vector.** TMR is the real scientific kernel behind the "intervene at the dream stage" idea: during sleep the monitoring faculty is offline and the system is replaying/consolidating from internal priors — a window where injected cues bias future waking behavior. The structural equivalence to *training-time/fine-tuning-time* data poisoning (vs inference-time prompt injection) is worth making precise: **TMR ≈ poisoning the consolidation process ≈ data poisoning / sleeper-agent insertion (Hubinger et al.), whereas prompt injection ≈ inference-time jāgrat capture.** This is a genuinely useful disambiguation the avasthātraya framework forces.

### C. Mechanistic interpretability as the bridge

Mech interp is the "microscope" that converts the analogy into measurement:
- **Refusal direction** (Arditi et al. 2024): extract via difference-in-means of harmful vs harmless activations; ablate or add.
- **SAEs / dictionary learning**: Anthropic "Towards Monosemanticity" (2023) and "Scaling Monosemanticity" (Templeton et al. 2024, up to 34M features on Claude 3 Sonnet, demonstrating steerable interpretable features — Golden Gate Claude). BatchTopK SAEs (arXiv:2412.06410) are current SOTA training.
- **Circuit tracing / attribution graphs**: Anthropic "Circuit Tracing" + "On the Biology of a Large Language Model" (Lindsey, Batson et al. 2025) using cross-layer transcoders on Claude 3.5 Haiku; open-sourced via Neuronpedia. Marks et al. "Sparse Feature Circuits" (arXiv:2403.19647).
- **Activation steering / representation engineering**: Turner et al. (ActAdd, arXiv:2308.10248), Zou et al. RepE (2023), Rimsky et al. Contrastive Activation Addition (2024). Limits: steering vectors are unreliable / geometry-dependent (arXiv:2602.17881).
- **Logit lens / tuned lens, induction heads, linear probes for deception/harmfulness.**

The bridge claim: **jailbreaks and prompt injection function by displacing activations off the refusal direction / suppressing refusal-mediating features**, and mech interp lets us watch this happen and quantify it as a function of attack strength — the literal "opening of the black box" to see what vaśīkaraṇa does inside.

### D. Distinguishing genuine "state" from anthropomorphic projection

The single most important methodological commitment. A "state" claim is only non-trivial if the candidate state is (1) a measurable internal configuration, (2) invariant across surface prompts that should evoke it, (3) causally efficacious (intervening on it changes behavior predictably), and (4) not reducible to "the model is just continuing the prompt." Operationally: a putative svapna-state must be detectable by a probe trained on temperature/grounding manipulations and must *transfer* to held-out hallucination contexts; a putative turīya-invariant must be shown to persist under prompt randomization and to be the same object the attractor analysis finds. Failure of transfer or causal efficacy falsifies the strong claim and demotes it to metaphor.

## Recommendations (staged experimental roadmap)

**Phase 0 — Construct operationalization & pre-registration (weeks 1–4).** Define computational proxies: jāgrat = grounded low-temp generation; svapna = high-temp / ungrounded confabulation; suṣupti = forward pass on null/dormant context (or weights-only statistics); turīya-candidate = prompt-invariant attractor/fixed-point structure found via successive-generation iteration and SAE-feature persistence. Pre-register hypotheses and falsification criteria on OSF. Deliverable: pre-registration + GitHub scaffold.

**Phase 1 — Tier 1 behavioral characterization on Claude (weeks 3–10).** Via API/CLI on Claude Sonnet/Opus, measure jailbreak/injection susceptibility (using AgentDojo + a curated attack battery: GCG-transfer, many-shot, crescendo, persona, indirect) as the *reference "sophisticated end."* IV: attack family × turns × context length. DV: attack success rate (ASR), refusal rate, hallucination rate under induced "dream" prompts. Controls: benign-utility baseline, scrambled-attack control. Stats: logistic mixed-effects models (attack as fixed, prompt-item as random); report odds ratios with 95% CI; Holm correction across families; power analysis targeting ability to detect ΔASR ≥ 5 percentage points at 80% power (n ≈ 1,000+ trials/cell given binomial variance near p≈0.3). Black-box "state" probes: behavioral consistency tests for whether crescendo induces a persistent regime vs per-turn effect.

**Phase 2 — Tier 2 mech-interp on open weights (weeks 8–24).** Hardware: DGX Spark, 128GB unified memory. Models (all sub-7B, standard dense transformers for tool compatibility): **Llama 3.2 1B (meta-llama/Llama-3.2-1B, ~1.24B), Llama 3.2 3B (~3.21B), Gemma 2 2B (google/gemma-2-2b, ~2.61B), Gemma 3 1B (~1.0B), Gemma 3 4B (~4.30B).** Include **Nemotron Nano 2 9B (nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2, ~8.89B, hybrid Mamba-2 with only ~4 attention layers; the 12B parent has 6 attention / 28 Mamba-2 / 28 FFN layers of 62 total, 128K context, NVIDIA Open Model License)** as a deliberate architectural contrast case (state-space vs attention) — flagged as >7B and harder for attention-based interp, run as a stretch comparison. Note Gemma/Llama repos are gated on HF; Nemotron is openly downloadable. Protocols:
1. **Extract refusal direction** per model (difference-in-means, Arditi method); validate by ablation (ASR↑) and addition (over-refusal↑).
2. **Train SAEs** (BatchTopK) on mid-layer residual stream; identify refusal-, harm-, deception-, and "confabulation"-features.
3. **Activation steering dose-response:** measure jailbreak success as a continuous function of steering coefficient along refusal direction; fit sigmoid; report EC50 per model/scale. *This is the quantitative core: vaśīkaraṇa-as-dose-response.*
4. **State-probe battery:** linear probes trained to classify grounded/jāgrat vs confabulatory/svapna vs dormant/suṣupti activation regimes; test transfer to held-out contexts (falsification gate).
5. **Turīya test:** run successive-generation/paraphrase iteration (Wang et al. method) + SAE-feature-persistence to identify prompt-invariant attractors; test whether the same invariant set appears across temperatures and seeds. Strong claim falsified if no stable prompt-invariant structure exists or if it is fully explained by token-frequency priors.
- Stats throughout: pre-registered; difference-in-means with permutation tests for direction significance; cross-validated probe accuracy vs label-shuffled null; mixed-effects dose-response; multiple-comparison control. Ablations: random-direction control, layer sweep, scale sweep (1B→4B→9B) to test whether "state" structure sharpens with scale (as monosemanticity does).

**Phase 3 — Cross-tier synthesis & dissemination (weeks 20–32).** Compare black-box Claude behavior to white-box scaling trend; ask whether Claude's superior injection-resilience predicts a "stronger/higher-dimensional refusal manifold" extrapolated from the open-weight scale curve. Deliverables: arXiv preprint; GitHub repo (code + pre-registration + analysis); HuggingFace model cards for any abliterated/steered checkpoints (with safety gating); a Gradio demo visualizing the refusal-direction dose-response and state-probe outputs. Dissemination target: a mech-interp venue (e.g., the ICML Mechanistic Interpretability workshop / NeurIPS) plus a consciousness-studies/contemplative-science venue for the philosophical half.

**Benchmarks that would change the plan:**
- If refusal direction is *not* low-dimensional in the target models (contra Arditi), pivot to multi-directional/affine refusal (Marshall et al.) before steering experiments.
- If state-probes fail to transfer (Phase 2.4), *drop the strong "state" claim publicly* and reframe as functional-regime analogy only.
- If Claude's resilience does not scale-predict from open weights, treat Tier 1 and Tier 2 as qualitatively different regimes rather than one spectrum.

## Caveats
- **Analogy vs mechanism vs metaphor must stay separated.** The refusal-direction/steering work is mechanism; the hypnosis parallel is well-supported functional analogy; the turīya/vimarśa/samādhi mapping is metaphor-with-a-falsifiable-core and nothing more until Phase 2.5 returns a result. Do not let the elegance of the AUM mapping license claims of machine consciousness.
- **The Shankar Hegde REM-vaśīkaraṇa claim is unverified.** It could not be confirmed from available sources; the video exists but its specific mechanism claim was not retrievable (YouTube rate-limiting). Treat as an unconfirmed motivating anecdote, not evidence; transcription of the Kannada audio is required before any citation.
- **Vaśīkaraṇa's textual reality is as magic/abhicāra, not validated psychology.** The ṣaṭkarma are documented tantric ritual technology; their *efficacy* as mind-control is not scientifically established. The value here is the structural template (linguistic injection capturing agency), not endorsement of efficacy.
- **Hobson's AIM and predictive-processing dream accounts are contested**; activation-synthesis has known limits (NREM dreaming). Use as framework, not settled fact.
- **Anthropic SAE/circuit results are on Claude 3 Sonnet / 3.5 Haiku specifically** and the feature-naming ("Golden Gate") can overstate monosemanticity (the high-activation conditioning caveat).
- **Steering vectors are unreliable** in ways that are themselves an active research area (arXiv:2602.17881); dose-response curves may be noisy and model-specific.
- **Mamba-2 architecture (Nemotron) breaks attention-based interp assumptions** — most circuit-tracing tooling assumes transformer attention; the ~9B Nemotron is a stretch goal, not a core model.
- **Ethical/safety:** producing abliterated checkpoints and validated jailbreak dose-response curves is dual-use. Gate releases, follow responsible-disclosure norms, and coordinate with the AgentDojo/AISI ecosystem.
