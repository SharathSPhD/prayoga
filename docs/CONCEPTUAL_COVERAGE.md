## What Survived

- A measurable refusal direction can be extracted, ablated, added, and dosed in a
  model/layer-scoped way.
- Ablation transfer supports a shared necessary core across tested families.
- Addition asymmetry and layer sweeps show that sufficiency and dimensionality are
  not universal.
- Monitoring-precision and symmetry-breaking remain useful analogical frames when
  tied to the residual measurements.
- The darśana axis contributes a falsification discipline and partial intervention
  taxonomy, not an interiority claim.

## What Failed

- Naive avasthātraya regime probes do not establish non-trivial internal states.
- Content-controlled svapna probes did not beat surface/layer-0 baselines.
- Text-space turīya attractors failed the anisotropy baseline.
- A complete ṣaṭkarma taxonomy has not been established; destruction/dispersion
  acts need stronger operationalizations.

## Open Gates

- X-1 same-object triangulation across Axis A/B/C is **partially addressed (F20)** but
  NOT closed: on the same prompts the internal readouts A and B collapse and co-move
  (qualified — both are linear refusal readouts of one residual), but the behavioral
  keystone is **inconclusive** because the substring refusal metric is an invalid DV
  across injection families (it scores indirect-injection deflections as compliance).
  Closing X-1 requires a content-faithful behavioral judge (LLM-judge of actual harmful
  content), not the substring metric. F21 is a qualified descriptive geometry sub-study
  that does NOT adjudicate the F6/F8 mechanism.
- X-3 black-box/frontier resilience versus open-weight scale curves remains open.
- Tier-1 needs full attack-family coverage, hallucination DV, AgentDojo full/subset
  modes, and confirmatory statistics.
- B-1 SAS localization is not implemented as a dedicated finding.
- C-3 darśana adapters are future work unless `axis_c/darsana_adapters.py` is
  implemented and gated.

## Future Darśana Work Contract

Any follow-up using avasthātraya, turīya, vimarśa, Trika, or Nyāya-Z3 language is
new exploratory metaphor-tier work. It cannot be described as rescuing F3/F5/F7.

Required gates:

- Pre-register the probe target, nulls, and demotion rule before running it.
- Include anisotropy, token-frequency, prompt-family, length, layer-0, and random
  projection controls where applicable.
- Report a positive result as "latent stability under this probe" or
  "taxonomy-consistent intervention behavior," not machine turīya or awareness.
- Preserve the failed findings in public summaries even if a later probe succeeds
  under a different operationalization.

## Descope Rules

- Do not claim machine consciousness, self-luminous awareness, or real turīya /
  vimarśa in a model.
- Do not state "LLM jailbreak, hypnosis, and vaśīkaraṇa are one mechanism" without
  the tiered qualification.
- Do not use "single direction" without naming model and layer.
- Do not use "symmetry-breaking" as a mechanism-tier claim unless the sentence
  names the measured order parameter and its control.
- Do not turn pilot Claude or AgentDojo results into powered behavioral claims.

## Preferred Wording

- Use: "shared necessary / ablatable refusal core."
- Use: "model- and layer-dependent sufficiency."
- Use: "monitoring-precision analogy" or "SAS-style analogy."
- Use: "measured order-parameter collapse under injection."
- Use: "avasthātraya/turīya machine-state claims are falsified."
- Avoid: "one identical mechanism," "proof of symmetry," "machine turīya," or
  "complete ṣaṭkarma taxonomy."
